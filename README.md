# Crow DEX Aggregator

Crow DEX Aggregator is a high-performance liquidity routing and aggregation platform built for modern onchain trading. It is designed to help traders, integrators, and trading products discover efficient execution paths across fragmented decentralized liquidity.

Crow focuses on one thing: turning scattered DEX liquidity into a fast, clear, and reliable execution layer.

## Why Crow

Decentralized liquidity is spread across multiple venues, pool types, and pricing models. That fragmentation creates unnecessary complexity for wallets, bots, aggregators, and users who simply want the best possible route with minimal friction.

Crow DEX Aggregator solves that by unifying liquidity discovery, route evaluation, and execution-ready output into a single system that can be integrated into trading products and infrastructure.

## What Crow Does

- Aggregates liquidity across multiple decentralized exchanges
- Evaluates routes across diverse pool and market structures
- Surfaces competitive execution paths in real time
- Reduces the operational complexity of fragmented onchain trading
- Provides a foundation for trading interfaces, routing APIs, and execution systems

## Core Features

### Multi-DEX Liquidity Aggregation

Crow brings together liquidity from multiple venues into a single routing layer, making it easier to access broader market depth without manually stitching together integrations.

### Smart Route Discovery

Crow continuously evaluates possible trading paths and identifies high-quality routes based on execution efficiency, not just headline price.

### Execution-Oriented Design

Crow is built for real trading environments. Its output is meant to be practical, actionable, and ready to plug into products that care about speed and reliability.

### Real-Time Market Awareness

Crow is designed to work with live market conditions so route selection can adapt as liquidity, pricing, and venue conditions change.

### Integrator-Friendly Foundation

Crow can serve as the routing backbone for wallets, dashboards, market tools, and trading infrastructure that need a dependable DEX aggregation layer.
